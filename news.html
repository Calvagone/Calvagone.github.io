News

  • December 1st, 2024: Release of Campsisverse, the package manager of the Campsis suite that allows you to install and update all Campsis packages in one go.

  • November 29th, 2024: Package campsisqual is now open-source on GitHub. Contact Calvagone to receive the qualification suite of models and qualify your installation of the Campsis suite. Check out our section on Quality.

  • September 11th, 2024: Release of an updated version of e-Campsis pro allowing the generation of forest plots to explore the impact of covariates.

  • February 2nd, 2024: Release of an updated version of e-Campsis including a multitude of minor improvements that collectively improve the user-experience and functionality of e-Campsis.

  • December 20th, 2023: Release of e-Campsis pro! The professional version of e-Campsis is now available.
